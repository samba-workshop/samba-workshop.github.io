# Travel

Flights can be scheduled to São Paulo–Guarulhos International Airport (GRU) or São Paulo–Congonhas Airport (CGH).
From São Paulo, intercity buses or shuttle services can be arranged, which are an affordable and convenient mode of transportation to Santos. Travel time by bus is approximately 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on traffic. Alternatively, you may choose to rent a car in São Paulo.
Santos is located about 75 km (47 miles) from São Paulo, and the drive typically takes around 1.5 hours under normal traffic conditions.

# Accommodations

### Sheraton Santos Hotel
Well located, close to the beach and a shopping mall, in a lively and well-frequented area. The hotel is modern and recently built, offering comfort and convenience.

### Ibis Santos Gonzaga Praia
A standard Ibis hotel located directly across from the beach and near the “new city center,” providing easy access to both leisure and business areas.

### Hotel Parque Balneário Santos
Located in the new city center, this is one of the city’s oldest hotels. It has been fully renovated, combining traditional character with updated facilities and modern comfort.

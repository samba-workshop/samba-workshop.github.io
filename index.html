About SAMBA

Symbolic Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in Baixada Santista (SAMBA) is an international scientific meeting bringing together Brazilian and international universities, research centers, and companies working in symbolic AI & ML.

Modern scientific knowledge depends on rigorous, transparent methods. Formulating clear hypotheses, curating data, and modeling phenomena using mathematical and symbolic representations are critical steps in an iterative process of analysis and refinement. Understanding these AI models is vital to validating them and gaining deeper insights into the studied domains.

Co-organized by the Federal University of ABC, Toulouse Capitole University, and Autogenetics, the workshop features presentations from invited speakers and is open to the general public free of charge.

Getting to Santos

✈️ Airports

International and domestic visitors will fly into São Paulo. Guarulhos (GRU) is the primary international hub, while Congonhas (CGH) serves mostly domestic flights.

🚌 Ground Transportation & Driving

From GRU: Direct executive buses via Viação Cometa run frequently straight to the Santos bus terminal. Alternatively, private drivers can be requested for approximately R$ 400,00 (USD 80,00).

From CGH: Take a short taxi/Uber to the Jabaquara Bus Terminal. From there, extremely comfortable coaches depart for Santos every 15 minutes.

Car Rental: If you plan to rent a car, ensure your GPS routes you through the Rodovia dos Imigrantes (Imigrantes Road). This is the safest and most modern route to descend the mountains to the coast.

Local Guide & Gastronomy

Balance rigorous academic discussion with the natural beauty and rich flavors of the Brazilian coast.

🏖️ Active Tourism

Santos features the longest beach garden in the world, perfect for morning beach runs or scenic walks alongside the famous Muretas de Santos.

If you enjoy coastal photography or hiking, consider a day trip up the coast to Bertioga. The contrast between the Atlantic Forest mountains and the clear waters provides spectacular scenery.

🍽️ Dining

The Gonzaga and Ponta da Praia areas are dense with excellent culinary options, bridging traditional coastal seafood with modern gastronomy.

We highly recommend local bistros that elevate their dishes with robust regional ingredients. Look for menus featuring rich mushroom reductions, artisanal cheeses, and creative savory sauces—especially those utilizing high-quality local cachaça to perfectly glaze lean cuts or fresh Atlantic catches.
